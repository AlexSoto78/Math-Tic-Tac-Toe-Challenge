— Rules —

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe! Before making your move, you must solve a random math problem. Your task is to answer correctly, as an incorrect response will result in your turn being skipped. If you ever want to restart the game, simply click on the 'TIC TAC TOE' header.

The game alternates turns between "Player 1" and "Player 2". To make a move, correctly solve the presented math problem. The game will check your answer within a time limit. If you succeed, you can make your move; otherwise, your turn is forfeited.

Winning conditions involve achieving a row, column, or diagonal of your symbol ('X' or 'O'). If you win, a winner alert will be displayed, and the game will reset. If all cells are filled, and no winner is found, the game is declared a tie, prompting a tie alert and a reset.
